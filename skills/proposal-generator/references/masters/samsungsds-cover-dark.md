# Master Pack: samsungsds-cover-dark

## Purpose
Cover/section-break master using a stronger premium look while staying enterprise-safe.

## Core layout
- aspect ratio: 16:9
- background: dark navy or cinematic enterprise image with overlay
- title: large, left aligned or center-left
- subline: short and restrained
- meta: small line near bottom or under title block

## Palette
- dark navy base: #07111F
- panel navy: #0F1B2F
- text: #EEF4FF
- muted: #A8B8D8
- accent blue: #7AB3FF

## Typography
- H1: 44-52pt bold
- subline: 18-24pt
- meta: 12-14pt

## Component rules
- one clear headline per slide
- use background image only when it supports the message
- keep overlay strong enough for readability
- do not make body slides depend on this dark style

## Suitable slides
- cover
- section openers
- closing statement slide
